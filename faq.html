Orders & Payment

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders within Pakistan. All online payments are processed securely through our payment gateway with SSL encryption to protect your personal information.

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by entering this number on our courier partner's website, or by logging into your LUXE account and viewing your order history. If you haven't received tracking information within 24 hours of placing your order, please contact our customer service team.

We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact us immediately at hello@luxe.pk or +92 300 1234567. If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, we'll do our best to accommodate your request. Once shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and initiate a return if needed.

There is no minimum order amount at LUXE. However, orders above Rs. 5,000 qualify for free standard shipping. For orders under Rs. 5,000, standard shipping charges of Rs. 200 will apply.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days for most locations across Pakistan. We also offer express delivery (1–2 business days) for major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and Rawalpindi. Delivery times may be longer during peak seasons, public holidays, or for remote areas.

Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We're working on expanding our services to include international shipping. If you're interested in international delivery, please contact us at hello@luxe.pk and we'll notify you once this service becomes available.

Our courier partner will attempt delivery up to three times. If you're not available, they'll leave a delivery notice with instructions for rescheduling or picking up your package from the nearest courier office. You'll also receive notifications via SMS and email about delivery attempts.

Standard shipping is free for orders above Rs. 5,000. For orders under Rs. 5,000, standard shipping costs Rs. 200. Express delivery is available for an additional Rs. 350 and guarantees delivery within 1–2 business days to major cities.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original condition with all tags attached. Sale items, intimate apparel, earrings, and personalized products cannot be returned. For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on certain items.

To initiate a return, log into your account and go to your order history. Select the order you wish to return and click "Request Return." You can also contact our customer service team at hello@luxe.pk or +92 300 1234567. We'll provide you with return instructions and arrange for pickup if applicable.

Once we receive your returned item and verify it meets our return policy requirements, your refund will be processed within 7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Yes, we offer exchanges for different sizes or colors of the same item, subject to availability. To request an exchange, contact our customer service team. If you'd like a completely different product, we recommend processing a return for a refund and placing a new order to ensure you get exactly what you want.

Products & Sizing

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in centimeters and inches. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing your measurements to our size chart for the best fit. If you're between sizes, we generally suggest sizing up for comfort. You can also contact our customer service team for personalized sizing advice.

We make every effort to display colors as accurately as possible. However, due to differences in monitor settings and lighting conditions, actual product colors may vary slightly from what appears on your screen. Each product includes multiple images from different angles and lighting conditions to give you the best representation.

Care instructions are provided on the product tag and description page for each item. Generally, we recommend following the care label instructions carefully. For delicate fabrics and luxury items, we suggest professional dry cleaning. Proper care will help maintain the quality and longevity of your LUXE pieces.

Restock timelines vary depending on the item and our suppliers. You can sign up for restock notifications on any out-of-stock product page, and we'll email you as soon as the item becomes available again. For popular items, we recommend acting quickly when notified as they may sell out again fast.
