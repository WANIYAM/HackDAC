Returns Policy

30-Day Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. To be eligible, items must be unused, unworn, and in their original condition with all tags still attached. Sale items, intimate apparel, earrings, and customized products are final sale and cannot be returned. If you're unsure about an item's return eligibility, please contact our customer service team before purchasing.

Once we receive your returned item, we'll inspect it to ensure it meets our return policy requirements. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7 business days and credited to your original payment method. Please note that it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

To initiate a return, contact our customer service team at hello@luxe.pk or call +92 300 1234567. Provide your order number and the item(s) you wish to return. Our team will guide you through the return process and provide you with a return authorization number and shipping instructions. Please do not send items back without contacting us first, as unauthorized returns may not be processed.

Yes! We offer exchanges for different sizes within 14 days of delivery. Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange. Please note that exchanges are subject to stock availability. If the desired size is not available, we'll process a full refund instead. Exchanges for different items or colors are not available — you'll need to return the original item and place a new order.

We sincerely apologize if you received a damaged or defective item. Please contact us immediately at hello@luxe.pk with your order number and photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund right away. For damaged items, return shipping is completely free, and we'll process your replacement or refund as a priority — typically within 3 business days of receiving your return.
