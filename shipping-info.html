Shipping FAQ

Standard shipping typically takes 3–5 business days for most locations across Pakistan. For major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, we offer express delivery which takes 1–2 business days. Please note that delivery times may vary during peak seasons, public holidays, or for remote areas. Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days.
